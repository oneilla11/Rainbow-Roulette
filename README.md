# Rainbow Roulette

![image](public/images/rainbow_roulette.png)

#### BY: Alex O'Neill

_____________________________________________________________________


- Game requires with at least 2 players, but no more than 10. 

- Players will choose between 6 colored zones over an unlimited number of 20-second rounds.  

- Each zone will be randomly assigned an outcome, determining whether the players survive, get eliminated, or gain immunity to elimination in the next round.  

- The last player left standing is the winner. Game restarts 5 seconds after round is won.
